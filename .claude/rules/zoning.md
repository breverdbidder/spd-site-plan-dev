# Zoning Rules

## Brevard County Zoning Codes

### Residential
- **RS-1**: Single-family, 1 acre minimum
- **RS-2**: Single-family, 20,000 sq ft minimum
- **RS-3**: Single-family, 12,500 sq ft minimum
- **RS-4**: Single-family, 10,000 sq ft minimum
- **RS-5**: Single-family, 7,500 sq ft minimum
- **RS-6**: Single-family, 5,000 sq ft minimum

### Commercial
- **BU-1**: Limited retail
- **BU-2**: General commercial
- **BU-1-A**: Neighborhood commercial

### Industrial
- **IU**: Light industrial
- **IU-1**: Heavy industrial

## Setback Requirements

Vary by zoning. Must query county GIS for specific parcel.

| Zone | Front | Side | Rear |
|------|-------|------|------|
| RS-1 | 35 ft | 15 ft | 25 ft |
| RS-2 | 30 ft | 10 ft | 20 ft |
| RS-3 | 25 ft | 7.5 ft | 15 ft |

## Allowed Uses

Query the county zoning database for specific allowed uses per zone.

## Variances

If project doesn't meet setbacks:
1. Document hardship
2. Prepare variance application
3. Submit to Board of Adjustment
