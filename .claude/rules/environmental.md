# Environmental Rules

## SJRWMD (St. Johns River Water Management District)

### When Permits Required
- Any project disturbing >1 acre
- Work within 100-year flood zone
- Wetland impact
- Stormwater management systems

### Permit Types
- **ERP**: Environmental Resource Permit
- **WUP**: Water Use Permit
- **Works of District**: Construction in/near waterways

## Flood Zone Designations

| Zone | Description | Building Requirements |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| X | Minimal risk | Standard construction |
| A | High risk, no BFE | Elevation required |
| AE | High risk, BFE known | Build above BFE |
| VE | Coastal high hazard | Strict requirements |

*BFE = Base Flood Elevation

## Wetland Assessment

1. **National Wetlands Inventory (NWI)** - Initial screening
2. **On-site Delineation** - Professional survey if wetlands suspected
3. **Mitigation** - If wetland impact unavoidable

## Environmental Checklist

- [ ] Check FEMA flood maps
- [ ] Query NWI for wetlands
- [ ] Review SJRWMD pre-application
- [ ] Assess stormwater requirements
- [ ] Document endangered species (if applicable)
